Most hyped right now

YEEZY

When Kanye West teamed up with adidas to design the Yeezy the world went mad. The Yeezy Boost 350 Turtle Dove released and sold out instantly online. YZY has since released the 750 Boost and the 950 Boot both of which were extremely popular. Fan favourites are arguably the Pirate Black and Turtle Dove colourways with Moonrock coming up close. The next major release is the Yeezy Boost 350 V2 which launched the debut Grey / Orange colourway. Other V2 editions have been released at Christmas of 2016. If you are looking for confirmed information on all the lateste Yeezy, raffle listings and all the action for the Yeezy range then this is the site for you.

aadidas Ultra Boost 4.0 Triple Black

The adidas Ultra Boost 4.0 Triple Black just went live early across the listed retailers. Official images are yet to be issued for this colourway which would suggest a wider release isn’t happening for some time. That said, you’ll want to cop now and avoid a painful L when these arrive and sellout across UK retailers. The Ultra Boost 4.0 is characterised by a thicker textured Primeknit which extends across the entire shoe. This takes on a full shade of black with lighter hits of grey visible across the lined imprint. The Triple Black Ultra Boost 4.0 is complimented with a soft nylon lace surround. It’s a unique feature which is yet to be seen on this performance runner. At the rear sits a 3D printed heel cap. All the features match the upper in a tonal shade of black. That even extends onto the Boost Unit underfoot. As always, you can be assured of re-energising cushioning with every stride. The adidas Ultra Boost 4.0 Triple Black won’t be on shelves for long. If you miss out on this early release, stay glued to our social media pages for news of the wider launch. Otherwise, take a look at more Ultra Boost 4.0 colourways via our ‘Release Dates’ page.

NIKE VAPORMAX

After 6 years in the making, Nike launch the revolutionary VaporMax – the worlds first exposed Air Bubble on the sole of a trainer. Made for runners and lifestyle enthusiasts the VaporMax provides modern comfort like no other shoe in the Air Max range. The FlyWire cables pull your foot in tight and make the sneaker hug your foot whilst the extra large bubbles cushion your step like never before – these must be tried on. Debut colours include the Pure Platinum edition and the OG University Red but there are more great colourways to come including the Triple Black and Triple White for the summer. Sneakerheads will definitely want to add these to their collections and do not worry, the bubble does not pop easily – they are super durable.
