"OFFWHITE"

OFF-WHITE is a fashion label founded by DJ and fashion designer Virgil Abloh in 2012, following his reappropriated art experiment, PYREX VISION. The current designs range from patchwork to graphic t-shirts, and all of them feature the company’s now trademark stripes, noticeably embossed on its back along with its name “WHITE.” As well as, being carried by various retailers worldwide, OFF-WHITE opened its first location and flagship store in Hong Kong.

SUPREME

Founded in 1994 by James Jebbia, Supreme is an iconic streetwear label that has gone on to amass a cult-like following with skaters, artists and collectors, whom are eager to get their hands on the brand’s latest drop. Supreme is also known for its extensive collaborations with Visvim, Comme des Garçons, Dover Street Market, Stussy, Nike and Vans.

BAPE

A Bathing Ape, also known as BAPE, is a Japanese streetwear brand founded in 1993 by NIGO. It encompasses men’s, women’s and children’s clothing, as well as a variety of accessories and lifestyle items. In 2011, it was announced that Hong Kong-based fashion conglomerate I.T Group purchased the brand for $2.8 million USD, and by 2013 NIGO had stepped down from the brand’s creative helm.

STONE ISLAND

Stone Island is an Italian brand founded by designer Massimo Osti in 1982. The brand specializes in the surface treatment of fabrics and dyeing treatments for a range of durable sportswear garments. Adhering to a principle of research, experimentation, and function, Stone Island’s production techniques are renowned for both their aesthetic and functional qualities.
